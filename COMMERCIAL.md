# Commercial / Enterprise Licensing

Cricket-Brain offers an enterprise licensing path for organizations deploying
neuromorphic sensing in regulated, mission-critical environments.

## Why Enterprise License

### 1) Legal & Procurement Readiness
- Commercial terms suitable for enterprise procurement workflows.
- Optional legal indemnification terms (subject to agreement).
- Defined support obligations and maintenance windows.

### 2) Priority Engineering Support
- Direct escalation path to maintainers.
- Architecture and deployment reviews for production rollouts.
- Guidance for mixed-language integration (C, Python, WASM/TS).

### 3) Performance & Hardware Optimization
- Custom tuning for target hardware profiles.
- Deterministic runtime profiling for latency-critical pipelines.
- Optional optimization engagements for embedded/edge deployments.

### 4) Compliance-Oriented Delivery
- Privacy-first telemetry posture for sensitive domains.
- Support for reproducibility and auditability requirements.
- Security and supply-chain guidance aligned with CI audit gates.
