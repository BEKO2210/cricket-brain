## Why You Need a Commercial License

The AGPL-3.0 requires that **any software that incorporates CricketBrain**
must also be released under AGPL-3.0, including source code. This applies to:

- SaaS and cloud services accessible over a network
- Proprietary products distributed to customers
- Embedded devices shipped with CricketBrain inside
- Internal tools within organizations that do not wish to open-source

If any of these apply to you, **you need a commercial license**.
